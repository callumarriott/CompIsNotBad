Why RFID Chips Are Great

Some great applications of RFID chips:

  • RFID poker chips track spending and prevent theft
  • Allows contactless payment to speed up transactions
  • Used to identify lost animals
  • Allows companies to uniquely identify their products and deter counterfeits
  • RFID chips are used in factories to track times taken for manufacture and to improve efficiency
